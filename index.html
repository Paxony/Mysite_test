Matplotlib plots examples


We prove many new medicines for cancer treatments by the health ministry. In our experiments we gave the medicine to mice by 45 days and review the tumor every X period to understand the changes and the effectiveness of the new medicine.
In this first table you can appreciate the survival rate. In the “x” line we plot the treatment duration (45 days), and in the “y” line you can match the survival rate. After a while you can identify Capomulin (red) like the best survival rate option in the treatment.
